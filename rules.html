Discipline


  • Great emphasis is laid on discipline and character building of students
  • A sense of pride and discipline is to be maintained at all levels by the students inside and outside the School Campus.
  • Students' co-operation is solicited for keeping School campus clean and tidy.
  • If any property /equipment in the Campus is damaged, the loss caused to the Institution shall be recovered from the concerned individuals.
  • Students are not allowed to bring Motor Cycle/ Scooter in the School and also not allowed to park Motorcycle/ Scooter in the neighbouring road side of the School to attend School classes.
  • Students are barred from bringing mobile phones in the School. Mobile phones found in possession of a student will be confiscated.

School Examinations


UNIT TEST, MID-TERM EXAMINATION & TERM-END EXAMINATIONS are carried out by the School as per the Examination Schedule issued to the students at the beginning of the session along with the Class Routine. A Merit Register is maintained to record the score of the students in all the above examinations. A student is required to score a minimum mark in the subjects as decided by the School Authority from time to time to justify his / her progress of studies in the subjects. An overall assessment of the performance of a student in all the examination(s) and Unit Tests held till the time of filling up of the Final/ Board Examination forms is made by the School authority and the students with unsatisfactory progress are not sent up for the Final/ Board Examination. Evaluated answer scripts of all the School Examinations i.e, Unit Tests, Mid-Term & Term-End Examinations are handed over to the students for appraisal of the parents / guardians.

Class Routine & Holiday List


The academic calendar is designed with the aim of having maximum numbers of working days as far as practicable. A copy of which together with the Class Routine and Examination Schedule shall be handed over to every student at the beginning of the session.

Identity Card


  • Identity Card will be issued to every student and this must be displayed in person while in the School Campus.
  • Any loss of identity card will be immediately reported to Administrative Officer in writing together with a stamp size photograph for issue of a fresh one with a penalty.

School Uniform


  • Wearing of School Uniform is a must for each and every student of the School.
  • The Colour and design of the Uniform will be specified at the time of admission.
  • Students not properly dressed in Uniform will be denied entry in the School campus.

Attendence


  • Regular Attendance is must for each and every student.
  • Non-Attendance of classes by the students is viewed seriously by the School Authority and actions as deemed fit will be taken against the defaulting students
  • Unless a student attends a minimum number of classes held in each subject as fixed by the Authority, the student shall not be allowed to appear in the Final/ Board Examinations.

Notice Board


Students are advised to note carefully all the relevant notices regarding Admission, Attendance, Examinations, Extra-curricular activities and other matters displayed on the School Notice Board and to act accordingly. Ignorance of any information / order displayed in the School Notice Board will not be considered as an excuse.

For Parents/Guardians


  • Parents /Guardians are earnestly requested to extend their active cooperation to the School Authority.
  • In order to acquaint themselves with the progress of studies, attendance and performances of their wards in the Unit Tests and Terminal Examinations, parents and guardians of 1st Year/ 2nd Year students along with their wards shall meet the Director/ Administrative Officer on any working day between 1:00 PM and 4:00 PM once in every month beginning from August/ July respectively till the end of the session.
  • Evaluated answer scripts will be handed over to the students for appraisal of their parents/ guardians who will exert their influence to bring the defaulters, if any, to the right track.
