Notice




Welcome


At Universal Knowledge Park School , Silchar , we try our best to instill the educational and co-educational experience that is high class in every respect and which prepares children for ideal citizenship. We believe that every child is unique . Every child is allowed to grow as an individual and thereby explore his / her full potential. Through our innovative techniques we strive to expose our students to stimulating and enriching learning experience .

Mission

Our mission is to foster each child's well-being as a foundation for academic and life success by drawing on the strengths of the child's entire community. To provide and teach the use of modern tools, materials and technology and by applying play-way method .

Our Goals

To provide a safe, secure and joyful environment in which each child can dare to dream, enjoy learning, achieve excellence and become a life long learner. To provide opportunities for children to step beyond the confines of classroom learning and the immediate context to explore new places and adventures. To inculcate in children , the value of compassion, empathy and respect towards other and the environment To encourage children to develope the qualities of self discipline , positive self image , team work , spirit of adventure and leadership . To inspire young minds to think creatively and laterally, solve problems and develop a lifelong enthusiasm for learning, To make children aware and appreciative of their history, cultures and traditions , whilst being open to other culture and alternative views of the world .
