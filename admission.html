General Admission Guidelines:

  • 1. Admission :

    • Students will be admitted on the basis of their merit.
    • Students securing less than a certain percentage of marks (to be fixed by the Admission Committee) in the Admission Test are NOT ELIGIBLE to apply for Admission.
    • Admission form along with prospectus can be obtained from the School Office during working hours on payment of requisite fees in specified dates and time.
    • Presence of Parents / Guardians along with the candidates is a must at the time of Admission.
    • Students must abide by the Schedule of Admission. The Schedule of Admission etc. will be notified in the School Notice Board.
    • Facilities extended by "Universal Knowledge Park " FOR POOR BUT MERITORIOUS STUDENTS: Poor but meritorious students with high percentage of marks and having a strong bent of mind to pursue higher education in science will be considered for monetary help by the "Universal Knowledge Park , Modhurbond Silchar ".

  • 2. Admission Fee :

    • Admission fees and Annual Charges will have to be paid at the time of Admission. The details will be notified in the School Notice Board.

  • 3. Documents Required For Admisson :


    Student are directed to enclose the copies of the following documents along with the Application Form for Admission and to produce the Original Copies at the time of Admission for verification.

    • 3 copies of recent passport size coloured Photographs.
    • Photocopy of Mark sheet of the Last examination passed.
    • Birth Cirtificate.
    • School Certificate (Pass Certificate).
